Exploring and learning all the different aspects of game development has been my main task in the last years, working in different projects with varied team sizes, making my own personal projects and developing prototypes to make my ideas become true. Prototypes

Projects developed in different game engines, coded with Blueprints in Unreal Engine 4 or C# in Unity. Game design and programming in all projects as main task, with level design and audio design in select projects.

Bandit Trail - UE 4 - Youtube Link

Infinite runner shooter in third person. Procedural world generation, “WANTED” progression system, character and weapon unlocks. In-game shop, characters with unique abilities (quick reload, Gatling gun, golden bullets), different game modes, power ups and power downs.

Run N Gun - Unity - Youtube Link

FPS with parkour elements (wall run, mantle, slide). Unlockable weapons through max score. Procedural recoil (visual and mechanical) and weapon sway movement. Roaming AI with predefined movement sectors. Dynamic AI spawns depending on enemy type.

3rd Person Hack & Slash Shooter - UE 4 - Youtube Link

Action/Adventure in Third Person Perspective, agile combat, weapons with unique abilities & per-weapon animations, targeting system and pick-up objects. Third person shooter mechanics combined with close quarters melee combat.

AI Test for Twin Stick Shooter - UE 4 - Youtube Link

Enemies try to stay within a set distance threshold from the player. When inside this threshold, the armed enemies start shooting at the player while also strafing right and left around the player character. Robot enemies move towards the threshold, at this point they select different random locations to plant mines that explode after a set number of seconds. After planting all mines, they rush towards the player to auto destruct. Enemy waves that increase in numbers progressively. Enemies have a random chance of dropping health or weapons. Weapons have a set number of seconds of use, before they are swapped to the starting rifle.

Down The Depths - Unity - Youtube Link

Action/Adventure in First Person Perspective, melee combat, character abilities (frost ray magic/area & healing effect). Bosses with unique abilities, AOE damage attacks with randomized locations, timed special attacks when the player gets too close or too far away.

Hide & Seek - UE 4 - Youtube Link

Hide & Seek concept. Third Person Shooter, the objective is to find several humans hidden inside a housing area which run away and hide when in visual or acoustic contact with the player. Artificial Intelligence developed with Behaviour Trees and Environment Query System (EQS). Heat wave vision to detect enemies through walls. Limited heat wave vision use, regeneration by eliminating enemies.
